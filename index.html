Enhance Assessment Accommodations for All Learners (Online Course)

Do you want to ensure all your students have a fair chance to showcase their learning? This intensive 2-week online course, Enhancing Assessment Accommodations for All, equips educators with the knowledge and skills to effectively implement accommodations for a variety of student needs


A certificate of completion as certified accommodations provider: reader and scribe

Learn more about this course

Introduction to Week 1

This course dives deep into the use of accommodations for:

  • Handwriting: Explore strategies to support students who face challenges with written tasks.

  • Reader and Scribe Services: Learn how to leverage reader and scribe support to ensure assessments are accessible for all

  • ICT and Assistive Devices: Discover how to integrate technology and assistive devices to create inclusive assessment experiences.

Instructor-Led Module

Module 1: Introduction to Assessment Accommodations

The module offers an overview of concepts, strategies and techniques used to ensure fair and accurate assessment for diverse learners. We explore the principles behind accommodations, policy documents, legal and ethical considerations, and practical implementation in educational settings. Participants will gain insights into tailoring assessments to meet individual needs, promoting inclusivity, and fostering equitable learning outcomes.

Starts Immediately

Instructor-Led Module

Module 2: Handwriting Accommodations

This module offers insight into reasons for handwriting accommodation, challenges faced by learners and practical implementation strategies. Explore diverse needs, alternative expression methods, and assistive technology to create an inclusive learning environment.

Starts Immediately

Instructor-Led Module

Module 3 and 4: Reader and Scribe Accommodations

These modules provide essential insights and practical guidance for implementing these accommodations effectively. Explore the rationale behind reader and scribe support, legal considerations, and best practices for implementation. Participants will learn strategies for selecting appropriate readers or scribes, ensuring equitable access to assessment materials, and promoting student success in diverse learning environments.

Starts immdeiately

Introduction to Week 2

This course dives deep into the use of accommodations for:

  • Confidence in Implementing Accommodations:: Master a practical framework for selecting and implementing appropriate accommodations for diverse learning needs.

  • Real-World Scenarios:Analyze real-world assessment scenarios and collaboratively explore effective accommodation strategies with fellow educators.

  • Technology Integration:Learn how to leverage technology tools to create accessible and engaging assessments.

  • Improved Student Outcomes:Equip yourself to ensure all your students can demonstrate their learning potential to the fullest.

Instructor-Led Module

Module 5: Introduction to ICT and Assistive Device Accommodations

The module is a comprehensive overview of essential technologies for educational support. Participants will explore the functionalities of ICT tools and assistive devices and how irregularities can be overcome.

Module 6: Using ICT and assistive devices for Reading Comprehension, and ICT Tools for Written Expression

This module covers the practical applications in assessment accommodations using ICT and assistive devices and empowering educators to leverage technology effectively to meet diverse learner needs and promote inclusive learning environments.
